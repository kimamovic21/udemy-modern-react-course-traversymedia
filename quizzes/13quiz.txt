Quiz 13: Section quiz


1. What is the primary characteristic of a headless CMS?

Answer: It allows managing content separately from its presentation


2. Why might someone choose a headless CMS over a traditional CMS like WordPress?

Answer: They offer flexibility with frontend technologies


3. In Strapi, what is the purpose of creating Content Types?

Answer: To define structured data models like blog posts or products


4. What is Neon?

Answer: A serverless Postgres database platform


5. What package must you install in the backend to connect Strapi with a Postgres database?

Answer: pg


6. Why is a generic type (<T>) used in StrapiResponse<T>?

Answer: To allow reuse for different data types returned from Strapi
